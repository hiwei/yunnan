Wander in Yunnan

Erhai Lake

Winds of Xiaguan, flowers of Shangguan, snow of Mt. Cangshan, and the moon of Erhai Lake. Lying against Mt. Cangshan, Erhai Lake possesses great charms especially with a Moon Scene.

Pray

The concept of the prayer wheel is turning the wheel of Dharma. The biggest one is in Shangri-la, and everything seems peaceful here.

Tiger Leaping Gorge

Legend says that in order to escape from a hunter, a tiger jumped across the river, hence the name.

Ji Gong Mountain

When a famous writer first visited here, he said 'this place definitely is as marvelous as Grand Canyon.' And the lake next to it is just like a wonderland in the world.

Songzanlin

In 1679, Dalai Lama chose this place by divination and become the uppermost public place in Yunnan. Annually, the Gedong Festival is celebrated here by the Tibetans.
